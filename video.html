Gemini Strike

Blast your way through enemy ships, evade fiery asteroids, sneak through blockades and face off against epic bosses in this action packed space shooter! Then when the blasting is done, raid cargo ships to find procedurally generated items to customise your fighter.

With a zillion different modules to mix and match (shields, missiles, lasers, beams, healing, EMPs) you are never short of ways to turn squads of enemies into beautiful cosmic fireworks.
